## English

kintore ("the App") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the App.

### 1. Developer

Developer: ottoto
Contact: clientcenter99@gmail.com

### 2. Information the App Collects

When you download and use the App, the following information may be collected:

- Your device's Internet Protocol address (e.g. IP address)
- The pages of the App that you visit, the time and date of your visit, the time spent on those pages
- The time spent on the App
- The operating system you use on your mobile device
- Workout settings and app preferences stored on your device
- Subscription status required to provide premium features

The App does not gather precise information about the location of your mobile device.

The App does not use Artificial Intelligence (AI) technologies to process your data or provide features.

### 3. Local Data Storage

The App stores workout data, settings, and app preferences locally on your device in order to provide its functionality.

### 4. Subscriptions

The App offers auto-renewable subscriptions through Apple's in-app purchase system. Purchase processing is handled by Apple. The developer does not directly collect your payment information.

### 5. Advertising

The App may display advertisements using Google AdMob. Google and its partners may collect data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

More information:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

### 6. Third Party Services

Only aggregated, anonymized data is periodically transmitted to external services to aid the developer in improving the App. The developer may share your information with third parties as described in this policy.

The App uses the following third-party services, each with their own Privacy Policy:

- [AdMob](https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543?hl=en)
- [Google Analytics for Firebase](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy)
- [Firebase Crashlytics](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy/)

The developer may disclose collected information:

- as required by law, such as to comply with a subpoena or similar legal process;
- when necessary in good faith to protect rights, safety, or to investigate fraud;
- with trusted service providers who work on the developer's behalf and have agreed to adhere to this policy.

### 7. External Communications

The App may access external network resources for the following purposes:

- Loading announcements
- Loading ads
- Verifying subscription-related information through Apple services

### 8. Opt-Out

You can stop all collection of information by the App by uninstalling it using the standard uninstall processes available on your mobile device or via the app marketplace.

### 9. Data Retention

The developer will retain collected data for as long as you use the App and for a reasonable time thereafter. To request deletion of your data, please contact clientcenter99@gmail.com and the developer will respond within a reasonable time.

### 10. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13. The developer does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If the developer discovers that a child under 13 has provided personal information, it will be deleted immediately. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information, please contact clientcenter99@gmail.com.

### 11. Security

The developer is concerned about safeguarding the confidentiality of your information and provides physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards to protect the information processed and maintained.

### 12. Your Choices

You may stop using the App at any time. You may also manage subscriptions from your Apple ID account settings.

### 13. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The latest version will be published on this page. Continued use of the App is deemed approval of all changes.

### 14. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact:
clientcenter99@gmail.com
